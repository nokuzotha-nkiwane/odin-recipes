Pizza Recipe

Designed by Freepik

Description

This is a recipe to make a fantastically delicious pizza from the comfort of your home! Since we value time we won't have you start from scratch. Make sure you have a ready made or store-bought pizza base and you're good to go.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sized pizza base of choice
  • 1⁄2 cup tomato paste (can be sauce)
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 3⁄4 tsp black pepper
  • chilli flakes
  • toppings of choice
    • pepperdew
    • olives
    • cheese
    • gherkins
    • cooked mushrooms
    • pepperoni
    • cooked mince
    • ham
    • parsely

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250°C.
  2. Place pizza base in oven proof tray or dish.
  3. Place a layer of tomato paste/sauce on pizza base.
  4. Add cheese as base topping.
  5. Add toppings of choice,as desired, over cheese.
  6. Season pizza with salt, pepper and chilli flakes.
  7. Place in the oven at 250°C for 20-30 minutes.
