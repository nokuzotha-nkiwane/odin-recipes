White Sauce Pasta Recipe

Designed by Freepik

Description

This is a recipe to make a fantastically delicious pasta with white sauce from the comfort of your home! This recipe works well for small get-togethers and even formal dinners. Hope you enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 3⁄4 tsp pepper
  • 500g pasta
  • small tray of mushrooms (400-500g)
  • 250ml water
  • parsely
  • 1L milk
  • 125g cake flour
  • 1 cup cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. While pasta cooks, cut mushrooms into slices.
  3. Saute mushrooms in a sauce pan.
  4. Add 1⁄2 tsp of salt and 3⁄4 tsp pepper to season mushrooms.
  5. Once mushrooms are done, place them into a container for use later.
  6. In the pan that made the mushrooms, pour 400ml of milk and bring to boil.
  7. While milk heats, mix flour and water in a cup into a paste (the fewer the clumps, the better).
  8. Ensure you have the cheese nearby.
  9. When the milk boils, add the flour paste to it and stir.
  10. After the milk and paste have mixed well, add cheese and continue stirring.
  11. Stir until it simmers into a thick sauce.
  12. Add salt to season.
  13. To serve (add quanitities as desired):
    1. Place pasta in plate.
    2. Pour souce over pasta.
    3. Add mushrooms.
    4. Top off with parsley.
