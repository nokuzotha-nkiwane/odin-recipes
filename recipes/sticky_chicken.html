Sticky Chicken Recipe

Designed by Freepik

Description

This is a recipe to make a fantastically delicious sticky chicken from the comfort of your home! This easy to follow recipe is great as an appetiser or as part of a complete meal. Hosting should be easy, fun and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 3⁄4 tsp pepper
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 8 chicken wings
  • 1⁄3 cup soy sauce
  • 11⁄2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp barbeque spice
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients, except the chicken, in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add chicken and mix well
  3. (If you have time) let it marinade for 30 min to an hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 250°C.
  5. Place seasoned chicken in oven-proof dish.
  6. Place dish into the oven.
  7. Grill for 30 - 45 min, until golden.
  8. Remove from oven and transfer to serving dish.
  9. Sprinkle sesame seeds over chicken and enjoy.
